Forbes' list of the most valuable sports teams

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The world's most valuable sports teams, as ranked annually by the American magazine Forbes, include teams from association football, American football, baseball, and basketball. National Football League franchise Dallas Cowboys, valued at $4.2 billion, was named the world's most valuable sports team of 2017, becoming the first non-association football team to top Forbes' ranking since its inception in 2010. English club Manchester United (2010–12) and Spanish club Real Madrid (2013–15) have previously each been named the most valuable team three times.
